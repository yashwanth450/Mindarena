🧠 MindStriker

«Strike With Your Mind.»

MindStriker is a next-generation brain training and competitive cognitive gaming platform designed to help users improve their mental abilities while competing with players from around the world.

Unlike traditional IQ tests or puzzle websites, MindStriker transforms brain training into an engaging, competitive experience through XP, leaderboards, seasonal competitions, achievements, and daily challenges.

---

🌍 Vision

Our vision is to build the world's largest competitive brain-training platform where millions of users can sharpen their minds, measure their cognitive skills, and compete globally.

We believe your brain deserves training just like your body.

---

🚀 Features

🧠 Brain Games

MindStriker offers multiple categories of scientifically inspired brain-training games.

Current categories include:

- Reaction Speed
- Memory
- Pattern Recognition
- Logical Thinking
- Focus
- Visual Intelligence

Each game is designed to improve different cognitive abilities while remaining enjoyable and competitive.

---

⚡ Instant IQ

After completing selected challenges, MindStriker estimates an Instant IQ Score based on overall performance.

The score considers:

- Accuracy
- Speed
- Difficulty Level
- Consistency

This score is designed for entertainment and self-improvement rather than clinical diagnosis.

---

⭐ XP System

Every completed challenge rewards XP.

XP helps players:

- Unlock achievements
- Increase their profile level
- Qualify for seasonal events
- Improve leaderboard rankings

The better your performance, the more XP you earn.

---

🏆 Leaderboards

Compete against players through multiple ranking systems.

🌍 Global Leaderboard

Compare your performance with players from around the world.

📍 State Leaderboard

See where you rank among players in your own state.

Future updates will also include:

- Country Rankings
- Friends Leaderboard

---

⚔️ BrainWar Seasons

BrainWar is MindStriker's flagship competitive event.

Each season features:

- 30 consecutive days
- Daily brain battles
- One challenge per day
- Limited completion time
- Real-time rankings

Players compete for the highest score over the entire season.

Only the smartest and most consistent players will reach the top.

---

👑 Wall of Fame

The Top 10 players from every BrainWar season earn a permanent place in the MindStriker Wall of Fame.

Their achievements remain displayed even after the season ends.

This creates lasting recognition for exceptional performance.
